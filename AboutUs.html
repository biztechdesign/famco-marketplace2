Who We Are

More than just an equipment dealer

Established in the UAE in 1978, FAMCO is a member of the Al-Futtaim Group — one of the largest privately-owned business houses in the lower Gulf region. We provide heavy vehicles and machinery to a diverse range of industries including construction, manufacturing, oil & gas, and transportation.

Through our Used Equipment marketplace we extend that same rigour — FAMCO-certified inspections, genuine OEM parts, and aftersales coverage — to every pre-owned unit we sell.

Our Journey

Nine decades of moving the GCC forward

From a single showroom in Dubai to a regional powerhouse with operations in 20+ countries — here's how we've grown alongside the customers who built the Gulf.

1930
Al-Futtaim Group is founded
The Al-Futtaim family establishes its first trading business in Dubai, laying the groundwork for one of the region's most diversified conglomerates.
1978
FAMCO opens its doors
Al-Futtaim Auto & Machinery Company is incorporated to distribute heavy equipment across the UAE — starting a partnership with Volvo Trucks that continues today.
1995
Expansion into KSA & Qatar
FAMCO opens branches across the GCC, becoming the sole regional distributor for Volvo CE, Volvo Buses, and a growing roster of global industrial brands.
2010
200+ brand portfolio milestone
FAMCO's product range grows to include Bobcat, Yanmar, Ingersoll Rand, SDLG, and dozens more — serving construction, oil & gas, and logistics customers.
2020
Digital transformation
FAMCO launches WhatsApp Commerce, online configurators, and Arabic-first digital tooling to bring equipment buying into the modern era.
2025
Used Equipment Marketplace
FAMCO Approved launches — the GCC's first OEM-backed used-equipment marketplace, extending our standard of trust to the pre-owned market.
