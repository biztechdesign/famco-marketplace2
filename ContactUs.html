Before You Ask

Quick questions, faster answers

Most buyers have the same few questions — here's what our team gets asked every day. Can't find yours? Scroll back up and drop us a message.

How quickly will someone get back to me?

Every form submission goes straight to a FAMCO advisor during business hours (Sun–Thu, 8 AM–6 PM GST). We aim to respond within one business hour, and always within 24 hours on weekends.

Can I visit the yard to inspect equipment in person?

Absolutely — in fact we encourage it. Use the form to request a physical inspection slot at our Dubai, Riyadh, Doha, or Manama yards. We'll run the machine for you, share the inspection report, and walk you through the condition grading.

Do you offer finance or leasing on used equipment?

Yes. Through Al-Futtaim Finance we offer term loans and operating-lease structures for qualifying businesses across the GCC. Select "Finance & Leasing" as your inquiry type and we'll pair you with a specialist.

Is there warranty coverage on pre-owned machines?

FAMCO Approved units come with a minimum 3-month / 500-hour powertrain warranty, extendable up to 12 months. The exact coverage depends on the unit's grade (A / B / C) and service history — all listed on each listing page.

Can you ship outside the GCC?

Yes. We regularly ship to East Africa, South Asia, and CIS markets. Our logistics team handles customs documentation, inland freight to the nearest port, and container loading — just mention your destination in the message field.
